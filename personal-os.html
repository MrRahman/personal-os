02

Daily Rhythms

Three Claude-powered rituals that structure your days and weeks. Each reads your live data and produces actionable output.

Morning

Morning Plan

Claude reviews your calendar, tasks, inbox, and goals—then creates meeting notes with strategic intelligence.

  1. 1Pull calendar + scan Gmail, Slack, iMessage for uncaptured actions
  2. 2Auto-capture Readwise → Notion KB + triage inbox items
  3. 3Cross-reference actions against Todoist + today's meetings
  4. 4Surface waiting-on items (3+ days stale)
  5. 5Create meeting notes with Strategic Frame for exec meetings
  6. 6Surface active goals, approaching milestones, stale goals
  7. 7Propose time-blocked plan with focus blocks
Evening

Daily Reflection

Compare plan vs actual, close the action loop, sync meeting transcripts, and capture personal wins.

  1. 1Compare planned tasks vs completed—calculate completion rate
  2. 2Close action loop: check if morning's uncaptured actions were addressed
  3. 3Sync Otter transcripts → extract decisions, commitments, action items
  4. 4Detect open commitments + write to People notes
  5. 5Reschedule P1–P2 incomplete tasks (user confirms)
  6. 6Generate reflection + prompt: Highlight, Adjustments, Check-In scores
  7. 7Prompt personal wins (workout, family, networking)
Weekly

Weekly Review

Analyze the week, track goals, surface relationship gaps, aggregate decisions, and set next-week priorities.

  1. 1Aggregate task completion rate, meeting hours, focus hours
  2. 2Analyze daily reflection themes + chronic blockers
  3. 3Review KB captures (High/Medium/Low relevance)
  4. 4Relationship Radar—flag cold relationships by type
  5. 5Aggregate decisions made this week across all meetings
  6. 6Check goal progress vs milestones, flag stale goals
  7. 7Propose next-week priorities + update goal targets

Obsidian Vault

Eight interconnected sections. Every note links to others. Dataview queries make it queryable.

Daily/

  • Daily notes (YYYY-MM-DD.md)
  • Plan section
  • Completed & Incomplete
  • Reflection
  • My Reflection
  • Check-In scores
  • Personal wins

Meetings/

  • Context & Strategic Frame
  • Suggested Topics
  • Decisions & Action Items
  • Follow-ups
  • Transcripts with speaker labels

People/

  • Context & Meeting History
  • Key Topics
  • Open Commitments (I owe / They owe)
  • Dataview queries
  • Types: work, family, personal, networking, mentor

Resources/

  • KB items from Readwise → Notion → Obsidian
  • Summary & Key Insights
  • My Notes & Connections
  • Auto-linked to Topics

Topics/

  • Maps of Content
  • AI, Crypto, Finance, Career, Leadership, Tech + life
  • Auto-populated with Resources, Meetings, People

Projects/

  • Active work initiatives
  • Status, Key Decisions, Open Questions
  • Timeline & Dataview links
  • ai-transformation, treasury-integration, job-search, personal-os, + more

Goals/

  • Annual (YYYY.md) + Quarterly (YYYY-QX.md)
  • Work + Personal goals
  • Milestones with dates
  • This Week targets (auto-proposed by weekly-review)
  • Linked to Projects

Ideas/ + Weekly Reviews/

  • Ideas captured via /idea
  • Status: seed → developing
  • Weekly Reviews (YYYY-WXX.md)
  • Full retrospective analysis

Knowledge Pipeline

Content flows from capture to connected knowledge automatically. Readwise → Notion → Obsidian, with Claude triaging every item.

Capture → Ingest → Triage → Connect

Automated pipeline from raw content to connected knowledge.

Capture
  • Readwise Reader inbox
  • Save articles, videos, podcasts, tools
  • From any platform
Ingest
  • /capture pulls to Notion KB
  • Status = "Inbox"
  • Deduplicates by URL
Triage
  • Classify type, assign 1–3 topics
  • Auto-tag 1–5 tags, summarize
  • Extract insights, detect actions
Connect
  • Create Obsidian Resource notes
  • Auto-link to Meetings, People
  • Update Topic MOCs
